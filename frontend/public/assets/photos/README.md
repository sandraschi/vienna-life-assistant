# Vienna Life Assistant - Photo Assets

**CRITICAL: Only photos YOU upload will be displayed. No generic images, placeholders, or fallbacks.**

## Add Your Own Vienna Photos

When you add photos with these exact filenames, they will appear in the Vienna attractions.
**If no photo exists, the space remains blank - no generic images shown.**

### Main Attractions:
- `stephansdom.jpg` - St. Stephen's Cathedral Vienna
- `belvedere.jpg` - Belvedere Palace Vienna
- `hofburg.jpg` - Hofburg Imperial Palace Vienna
- `schonbrunn.jpg` - Schönbrunn Palace Vienna
- `prater.jpg` - Prater Park/Ferris Wheel Vienna
- `naschmarkt.jpg` - Naschmarkt Vienna
- `cafe_central.jpg` - Café Central Vienna
- `khm.jpg` - Kunsthistorisches Museum Vienna
- `albertina.jpg` - Albertina Museum Vienna
- `haus_des_meeres.jpg` - Haus des Meeres Vienna

### Optional Coffee Houses:
- `cafe_sperl.jpg` - Café Sperl Vienna
- `cafe_hawelka.jpg` - Café Hawelka Vienna
- `cafe_pruckel.jpg` - Café Prückel Vienna

## Requirements:
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: 1200x800px or larger recommended
- **Quality**: Your own photos of actual Vienna attractions
- **Naming**: Exact filenames above (lowercase, underscores)

## How to Add Photos:
1. Take photos of real Vienna attractions or find high-quality images
2. Save with the exact filenames listed above
3. Place files in this `frontend/public/assets/photos/` directory
4. Restart the application to see your photos

**Photos you don't upload will show as blank spaces - no generic or wrong images will ever be displayed.**
